Sophia's Recipe for Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Optional add-ins:

  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually mix dry ingredients into the butter mixture until well combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips and any optional add-ins.
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized balls of dough and place them onto a lined baking sheet.
  8. Leave enough space between each cookie as they will spread while baking.
  9. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  10. Let cookies cool on the sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
