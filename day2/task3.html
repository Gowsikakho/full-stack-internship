The Best Movies According to Gowsika

My top 3 favorite movies of all time

Inception

A mind-bending thriller that explores dreams within dreams. The concept and visuals are stunning.

Little Women (2019)

A beautiful adaptation of the classic novel. The characters and emotions feel very real and inspiring.

Interstellar

A visually stunning space epic that dives deep into love, time, and human survival. Incredible soundtrack too.
